• About Star Wars

    Star Wars was created by George Lucas in 1977 and it became a worldwide pop-culture phenomenon. It is now owned by Lucasfilm. Star Wars is a science-fiction franchise consisting of movies, books, comics, video games, toys, live action shows, and animated shows. It became one of the most successful and influential franchises in motion picture history.

  • About SpaceX

    SpaceX, also known as Space Exploration Technologies Corporation, was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk and it is headquartered in Hawthorne, California. SpaceX is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation services company which has the goal to reduce space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.
    It was the first private company to launch and return a spacecraft from Earth orbit and the first to launch a crewed spacecraft and dock it with the International Space.

  • About Dell Technologies

    Dell Technologies was founded by Michael Saul Dell in 1984 and on September 7, 2016 it merged with EMC Corporation. The company is headquartered in Round Rock, Texas.
    Dell is an American multinational technology company which engages in the provision of information technology hardware, software, and service solutions through its subsidiaries.
